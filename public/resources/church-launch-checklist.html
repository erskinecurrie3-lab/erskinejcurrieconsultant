Church Launch Checklist

Everything You Need Before Opening Day

Provided by Erskine J Currie Ministries

Critical
Important
Nice to Have

6+ Months Before Launch

  • Confirm your calling and vision — Have it affirmed by mentors and spiritual leaders
  • Identify your target community — Research demographics, needs, and existing churches
  • Build your core team — Recruit 15-25 committed individuals who share the vision
  • Secure initial funding — Develop a fundraising plan for 12-18 months of expenses
  • Establish legal entity — Incorporate as a nonprofit, obtain 501(c)(3) status
  • Open a church bank account — Set up financial systems and accounting
  • Develop a ministry plan — Sunday format, discipleship pathway, small groups
  • Begin meeting as a core team — Weekly gatherings for prayer, planning, and bonding

3-6 Months Before Launch

  • Secure a meeting location — Sign lease, negotiate terms, plan setup/teardown
  • Recruit worship team — Audition and begin rehearsals
  • Develop children's ministry plan — Curriculum, volunteers, safety policies
  • Build your website — Professional, mobile-friendly, with service times and location
  • Launch social media accounts — Begin building an online presence
  • Purchase equipment — Sound system, projector, instruments, children's supplies
  • Set up online giving — Choose a giving platform and configure it
  • Design logo and branding — Create a consistent visual identity
  • Order promotional materials — Banners, yard signs, invite cards, t-shirts

1-3 Months Before Launch

  • Host preview services — 2-4 practice runs with your core team and invited guests
  • Train all volunteer teams — Greeters, ushers, children's workers, tech team
  • Develop guest follow-up system — Connection cards, email sequences, personal calls
  • Plan community outreach events — Serve your neighborhood before asking them to attend
  • Finalize launch day logistics — Setup timeline, team assignments, contingency plans
  • Obtain insurance — General liability, property, workers' comp if applicable
  • Set up church management software — Database, communication, volunteer scheduling

Launch Week

  • Final team meeting and prayer — Cast vision one more time, pray together
  • Full dress rehearsal — Run through the entire service with all teams
  • Community invitation blitz — Door-to-door, social media push, personal invitations
  • Prepare welcome packets — Info cards, small gifts, connection cards for guests
  • Test all equipment — Sound, video, lighting, HVAC, WiFi
  • Confirm all volunteer assignments — Everyone knows their role and arrival time

Launch Day

  • Arrive early for setup and prayer — Team arrives 2+ hours before service
  • Execute with excellence and joy — Smile, be genuine, trust your preparation
  • Collect guest information — Connection cards, digital check-in, or both
  • Follow up with every guest within 24 hours — Email, text, or personal call
  • Debrief with your team — What went well? What needs improvement?
  • Celebrate! — You did it. Thank God and your team.
